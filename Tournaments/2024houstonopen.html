A 5-round tournament sponsored by F & B Trophies of Houston, TX that provides a great opportunity to practice your classical chess. More about it here: https://www.kingregistration.com/event/Houstonopen24

Tournament Format: 3-Day or 2-Day Schedules Available "OPEN" & "U2000" SECTIONS are FIDE RATED using FIDE Rules - 5SS, G/90, i30 (US Chess Ratings used for pairings) ALL OTHER SECTIONS - 5SS, G/120, d5. Game 1 of 2-Day Schedule is G/60, d5 for ALL sections. Game 1 of 2-Day Schedule is not FIDE rated for Open or U2000.

Prizes: Open: $1500 + plaque - $900 - $500 - $300 - $200 Top female in Open: $250 U2200 Open Class Prize: $700-400 U2000: $800 + plaque - $400 - $200 - $125 - $100 U1800: $800 + plaque - $400 - $200 - $125 - $100 U1600: $800 + plaque - $400 - $200 - $125 - $100 U1300: $600 + plaque - $300 - $200 - $100 U1100: $450 + plaque - $225 - $150 - $100 * Unrated players cannot play in U1100.
